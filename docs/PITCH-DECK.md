# Trust Agent - Pitch Deck Reference

**Operator:** AgentCore LTD - Company No. 17114811
**Website:** trust-agent.ai

## The Problem

AI companions exist, but none are verified for safety. Users have no way to know if an AI role is trustworthy, especially in sensitive domains like education, health, and elderly care.

## The Solution

Trust Agent - the world's first AI companion platform where every role is audited through a three-stage trust pipeline before deployment. 78 audited roles across 38 immersive environments, covering education, health, wellness, elderly care, children's content, legal/financial, and professional development.

## Market Opportunity

- UK EdTech market: £3.4B
- Global AI companion market: $12B by 2028
- NHS social prescribing: 1M+ referrals/year
- Enterprise AI training: $35B
